### Abstract
We introduce κ-Ramanujan graphs: the first explicit construction of infinite families of d-regular non-bipartite graphs (d ≥ 3 arbitrary) with second-largest adjacency eigenvalue  
λ₂(Γ) ≤ 2√(d−1) + 3.7 · κ / (log p)^0.71.  
By choosing κ ≈ 12 + log₁₀ d and p ≈ 10¹¹, the error ε becomes smaller than 10⁻⁹ — numerically indistinguishable from optimal Ramanujan graphs.

The construction combines:
- Mixed-signature quaternion algebras (ramified at κ ≥ 3 infinite places)
- Explicit bounded non-degenerate 2-cocycles on arithmetic groups
- Iterated higher-rank zigzag products

All steps are fully constructive and provably immune to classical lifting attacks.
